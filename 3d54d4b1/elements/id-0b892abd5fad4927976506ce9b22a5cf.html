Notify business of rejection if incorrect data is submitted ()
Description: Businesses to be notified if there is incorrect data. They have a deadline to provide the correct eInvoice following the nationally used format for eInvoices that include the necessary data needed to report at EU level. If a business fails to deliver the correctly formatted eInvoice, a notification must be provided to the business rejecting the eInvoice.
