Business Agnostic Digital Business Capabilities Map ()
Definition: Business Agnostic Digital Business Capabilities Map ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of digital business capabilities that are not specific to any particular industry or business. These capabilities encompass various aspects of digital business operations, including electronic identification, qualification management, digital payments, digital procurement, digital communication, digital exchange, digital invoicing, data management, multimedia management, contact management, case and workflow management, knowledge management, digital twin, and artificial intelligence. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Business Agnostic Digital Business Capabilities Map ABB is a comprehensive catalogue of digital business capabilities that are applicable across various industries and businesses. These capabilities are fundamental to the operation of a digital business and are not tied to any specific business or industry. They cover a wide range of areas, from electronic identification, which involves the use of digital methods to identify individuals or entities, to artificial intelligence, which involves the use of machines and algorithms to mimic human intelligence. Other capabilities include digital payments, digital procurement, digital communication, digital exchange, digital invoicing, data management, multimedia management, contact management, case and workflow management, knowledge management, and digital twin. Example: For instance, a business in the retail industry might use the digital payments capability to facilitate online transactions, the digital communication capability to interact with customers, and the data management capability to analyze customer data. Similarly, a business in the manufacturing industry might use the digital twin capability to create a virtual model of a physical product, the artificial intelligence capability to automate certain processes, and the digital procurement capability to manage its supply chain.
