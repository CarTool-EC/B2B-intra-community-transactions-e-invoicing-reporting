Delegation of Powers Provisioning Digital Public Services ()
Definition: Delegation of Powers Provisioning Digital Public Services ABB is an Assessment representing the extent to which the digital public service delivery is delegated to lower public administrative tiers or to private organisations acting on behalf of the public administration.
