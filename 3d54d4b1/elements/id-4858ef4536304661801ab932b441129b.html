Directive 2009/132/EC on the exemption from VAT on final importation of certain goods ()
Definition:This directive determines the scope of Article 143(b) and (c) of Directive 2006/112/EC concerning the exemption from value-added tax (VAT) on the final importation of specific goods. The directive provides clarity on the circumstances under which certain goods can be exempted from VAT upon their final importation.
