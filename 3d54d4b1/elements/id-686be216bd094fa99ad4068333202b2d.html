Digital sovereignty and autonomy ()
Description: Digital sovereignty means digital infrastructures, products and services that safeguard European security, strategic assets and interests while respecting European values and requirements. Additional information: European Institutions and Administrations are representing citizens and businesses and respond to and ensuring the proper development of European society in its most holistic manner. Therefore, there is a need of preventing to spend and depending on external stakeholders to foster the monopoly and vendor lock-in making non-efficient solutions and subject to external agents' desires. The Digital Strategy sets a new vision, addressing digital transformation opportunities of a post-pandemic scenario, and supporting the delivery of the EU’s strategic priorities by 2030.
