VAT rate applied ()
Definition: Percentage of the transaction value that is added as tax, based on the type of goods or services being provided and the jurisdiction in which the transaction occurs. VAT rates can vary significantly depending on the country, the nature of the goods or services, and specific conditions of the sale.
