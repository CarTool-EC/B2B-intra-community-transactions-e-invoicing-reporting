Master Data ()
Definition: Master Data ABB is a Data Object that enables the implementation of non-transactional information to play a key role in the core operation of a business in public administrations and re-used for multiple purposes Source: European Medicines Agency Source reference: https://www.ema.europa.eu/en/glossary/master-data#:~:text=Master%20data%20Any%20non-transactional%20information%20considered%20to%20play,product%2C%20organisation%20and%20referential%20%28SPOR%29%20master%20data%20%27.
