Directive 2006/112 on the common system of VAT ()
Definition: This directive clarifies EU's VAT legislation currently in force, and the obligations of taxable persons. This directive also allows for exemptions from VAT such as financial and insurance services, medical care or social services. EU countries had to incorporate it into national law by 1 January 2008.
