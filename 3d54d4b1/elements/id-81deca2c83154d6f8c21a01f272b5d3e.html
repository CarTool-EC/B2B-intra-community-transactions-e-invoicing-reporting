Allow national tax administration to access data of B2B intra-community e-invoices through the central VIES ()
Description: The appointed tax official(s) of every national tax administration is authorised to access the needed data from B2B intra-community e-invoices reported by other countries to verify the fiscal information. The information can be shared through push or pull notifications.
