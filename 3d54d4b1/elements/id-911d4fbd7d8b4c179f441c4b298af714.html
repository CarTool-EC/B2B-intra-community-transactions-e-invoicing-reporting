Information ()
Definition: Information ABB is a Business Object describing business facts, assets, or opinions that are exchanged in the context of a digital public service to support its delivery. Information is enacted via interpretation rules (expressed in an ontology) of data and considering knowledge of a given context. Examples include an invoice, a medical prescription, a driving license.
