Directive 2014/55 on guidelines for eInvoicing transmission ()
Definition: Directive 2014/55 EU mandates the implementation of an eInvoicing solution based on European standard. But the directive does not mandate any specific transmission system or transport infrastructure. skos:noteDirective 2014/55 EU mandates the implementation of an eInvoicing solution based on European standard. But the directive does not mandate any specific transmission system or transport infrastructure. European Commission has been promoting through the CEF and DIGITAL programmes the eDelivery Building Block, as the standard transmission and AS4 (exchange protocol), without imposing the use of a single eInvoicing system per se.
