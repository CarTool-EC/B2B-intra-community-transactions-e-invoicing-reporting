Directive 2019/770 on certain aspects concerning contracts for the supply of digital contents and digital services ()
Description: Directive 2019/770 is a legislation enacted by the European Parliament and Council on 20 May 2019 that focuses on specific aspects related to contracts for the provision of digital content and digital services. The directive aims to harmonize and strengthen consumer rights in the digital market by establishing clear rules and obligations for suppliers of digital content and services. It covers various aspects such as the conformity of digital content and services, remedies for non-conformity, the right to updates and upgrades, and the protection of personal data. The directive seeks to enhance consumer trust and confidence in the digital marketplace while promoting fair competition and innovation.
