PEPPOL ()
Description: OpenPeppol is a non-profit international association that manages the development and maintenance of the PEPPOL Network. PEPPOL is a set of standards for exchanging electronic business documents, such as invoices, orders, and delivery notes. The PEPPOL Network is a global network of organizations that provide access to PEPPOL services. Additional information: OpenPeppol is a non-profit organization that oversees the PEPPOL network, a global network that allows organizations to exchange business documents electronically. The PEPPOL network is based on a set of standards that facilitate the exchange of documents such as invoices, orders, and delivery notes. OpenPeppol's role is to manage the development and maintenance of this network, ensuring its smooth operation and continuous improvement. Example: A company can use the PEPPOL network to send an electronic invoice to a customer in another country. The invoice is sent through the network, which ensures that it is delivered in a format that the customer's system can process. This eliminates the need for manual data entry and reduces the risk of errors. Similarly, a supplier can send an electronic delivery note to a customer, who can then automatically update their inventory management system.
