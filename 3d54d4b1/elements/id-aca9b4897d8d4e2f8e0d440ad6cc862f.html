Interoperability Strategy ()
Definition: Interoperability Strategy ABB is a Goal that enables the ability of disparate and diverse organisations to interact towards mutually beneficial and agreed common goals, involving the sharing of information and knowledge between the organisations, through the business processes they support, by means of the exchange of data between their respective ICT systems. Thus, for example, the Interoperable Europe Act supports public sector innovation and public-private 'GovTech' projects. It sets up a new cooperation framework for interoperability in the public sector.
