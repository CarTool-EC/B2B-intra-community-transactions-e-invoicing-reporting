Tax Digital Public Service Providers Catalogue ()
Definition: Tax Digital Public Service Providers Catalogue ABB is a Grouping that covers a range of business actors involved in the provision of digital public services related to taxation. These include Tax Administration, Financial Service Providers, Property Management Authorities, Internal Affairs, and Central Liaison Offices. Source: EIRA Team Research Additional information: The Tax Digital Public Service Providers Catalogue is a comprehensive list of business actors that play a crucial role in the digital public services sector, particularly in relation to taxation. These entities are responsible for various tasks, such as administering tax laws, providing financial services, managing properties, handling internal affairs, and serving as central liaison offices. Each of these actors has a unique role and responsibilities, but they all contribute to the efficient and effective delivery of digital public services in the tax sector. Example: Tax Administration Business-actor is responsible for implementing tax laws and regulations, collecting taxes, and providing services to taxpayers. Financial Service Provider Business-actor offers financial services, such as loans, savings accounts, and investment products. Property Management Authority Business-actor manages public properties, including their maintenance, leasing, and sale. Internal Affairs Business-actor handles internal matters within an organization, such as employee relations, internal communications, and policy development. Central Liason Office Business-actor serves as a communication link between different departments or organizations.
