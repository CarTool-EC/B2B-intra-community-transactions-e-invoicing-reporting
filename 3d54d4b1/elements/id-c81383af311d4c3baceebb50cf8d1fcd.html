Management of access right/authorisation rights to the data (depending on type of data) ()
Description: Central VIES provides access and authorisation to restricted data to a determined body/persons according to pre-established business rules (authorisation rights and data governance) in compliance with european laws in order to assure the proper control over data collection purposes.
