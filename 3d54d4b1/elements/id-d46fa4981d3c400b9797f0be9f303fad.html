Digital First ()
Description: Public services are transformed into digital public services Additional information: Making public services digital makes them more accessible, reduces administrative burdens and time and allows faster responses to individuals and organizations. The European Commission has also set out a strategy for the digital transformation of its own services. Digital channels are also the Commission’s preferred way to communicate. The Digital Strategy sets a new vision, addressing digital transformation opportunities of a post-pandemic scenario, and supporting the delivery of the EU’s strategic priorities by 2030.
