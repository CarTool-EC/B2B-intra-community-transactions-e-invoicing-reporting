Address instances of VAT fraud ()
Definition: Value added tax (VAT) is a major source of revenue in all EU Member States. It is also a key source of financing for the EU budget. The revenue loss, known as the ‘VAT gap’, delineates the issues caused by sub-optimal VAT collection and control. A significant part of this loss is due to missing trader intra-Community (MTIC) fraud. ViDA intends to reduce this VAT fraud by digitalising the control of VAT fraud.
