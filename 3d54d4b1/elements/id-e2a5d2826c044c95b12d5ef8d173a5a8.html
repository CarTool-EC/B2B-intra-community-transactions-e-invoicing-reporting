Regulation 282/2011 implementing measures on the common system of VAT ()
Definition: The objective of this Regulation is to ensure uniform application of the current VAT system by laying down rules implementing Directive 2006/112/EC, in particular in respect of taxable persons, the supply of goods and services, and the place of taxable transaction.
