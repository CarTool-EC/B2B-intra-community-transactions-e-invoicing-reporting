Recommendation on a trusted and secure European e-ID ()
Description: The Commission Recommendation of 3.6.2021 on a common Union Toolbox for a coordinated approach towards a European Digital Identity Framework aims to establish a trusted and secure European e-ID system. It proposes a set of guidelines and tools to ensure the interoperability, security, and privacy of digital identities across the European Union. The framework emphasizes the use of decentralized and user-centric solutions, enabling individuals to have control over their personal data while facilitating seamless and secure access to online services. The recommendation also highlights the importance of international cooperation and standards to ensure the global recognition and acceptance of European digital identities.
