TVI-Access and Identity Infrastructure Enablers [Traceability] ()
Narrative: The Identity Access Infrastructure focuses on managing user identities and their access privileges. It encompasses the [Identity Provider Technology Service], which enables the secure identification and authentication of users, and the [Federated Identity Provider Technology Service], which allows seamless integration with external identity providers. Additionally, the [Lightweight Directory Access Technology Service] facilitates efficient storage and retrieval of user and access information. To strengthen security and privacy within the technical view, the access and identity infrastructure relates to various security application services. These services include robust encryption mechanisms, secure communication protocols, and threat detection and prevention systems. By integrating these security application services, the access and identity infrastructure ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of sensitive data and resources. Moreover, privacy enablers are incorporated into the access and identity infrastructure to comply with privacy regulations and protect user data. These enablers encompass mechanisms for data anonymization, consent management, and data retention policies. By implementing these privacy enablers, organizations can demonstrate their commitment to privacy protection and establish trust with their users. By extending the narrative to include the Technical view - access and identity infrastructure, organizations can establish a secure and privacy-conscious foundation for their digital solutions. This view leverages the Management Infrastructure Enablers and Identity Access Infrastructure, while integrating security application services and privacy enablers to safeguard sensitive information, ensure proper user authentication and authorization, and adhere to privacy regulations. By doing so, organizations can provide a trusted and secure environment for their users while maintaining compliance with relevant security and privacy standards.
