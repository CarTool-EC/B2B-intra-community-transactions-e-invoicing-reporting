OV-Digital Public Services Catalogue ()
NARRATIVE: The OV-Digital Public Services Catalogue view extends Organisational view and provides a comprehensive framework for leveraging the digital transformation of public services. The view is composed of two main elements: the "Digital Public Service Catalogue" and the "Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue". The "Digital Public Service Catalogue" is a Business Object ABB that enables the index of active digital public services, and their descriptions, provided by a public administration. This ABB is a key interoperability enabler for sharing and provisioning, as well as reusing and consuming of front-office public services. The "Business Agnostic Digital Public Services Catalogue" is a Grouping ABB that includes a variety of Business Service ABBs. These services range from ePayment, Audit, eArchiving, eDelivery, eQualification, eTranslation, Data Extraction, Transformation and Loading, Data Analytics, Delegation of Power, Workflow Management, Knowledge Management, eNotification, eIdentification, Digital Collaboration, Waiting list Management, Open Data Management, Anonymisation, eSignature, Secure Access Control, Content Management, Log Management, eInvoicing, eTendering, Contact Management, Forms Management, Data Exchange, Digital Inmutability, Business Intelligence, Compliance Testing, Data Space, to Data Space Participant. Each of these services represents a specific digital public service provided by the public administration. The view also includes a "Customs Digital Public Services Catalogue", which is another Grouping ABB. This catalogue includes Business Service ABBs that are specific to customs operations, such as Economic Operators Registry management, EU Customs Trader Portal, Analysis Management, (Internal) Customs logistics management, Centralised Clearance for Import, Determination of the economic value of goods declared for importation, Tariff Registry management, Applications and Requests, Compliance verification management, Duties guarantee management, Origin Registry management, Customs Decisions system, Customs organizational structure management, Duties calculation and payment, Partner authorization, Customs declarations, Procedure Automation System, Preservation of cargo control, Intra-EU purchases and sales, Notification of Arrival, Presentation Notification and Temporary Storage, Classification of goods, Procedures registry management, Tariff Suspensions, Computerised Transit, Risk analysis, Laboratories registry management, EU Customs information exchange, Goods flow analysis and simulations, and Economic Operator management and accreditation. In summary, the OV-Digital Public Services Catalogue view provides a comprehensive framework for understanding and managing the digital transformation of public services, with a particular focus on interoperability and the sharing and reusing of services.
